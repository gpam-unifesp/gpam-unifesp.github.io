GPAM — Machine Learning Research Group

GPAM is a research group based at the Federal University of São Paulo (UNIFESP) dedicated to advancing machine learning theory and applications, with emphasis on adaptive learning systems, automation, and data-driven discovery.

Our work spans Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), graph-based learning, evolutionary computation, data streams, semi-supervised learning, and predictive modeling for complex real-world problems.
